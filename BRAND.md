## 01 · BRAND POSITIONING

**Who we are**
Ventra is a B2B coffee equipment leasing business. Premium machines, specialty beans, and full-service maintenance under a single monthly agreement. Everything works, always. You never have to think about it.

**Positioning statement**
> For organisations that value their people, Ventra is the coffee partner that simply works.

**Tagline**
> Australia's managed coffee partner.

**Target sectors**
- International higher education (primary)
- Pathway colleges & private HE providers
- NSW Health & public sector (Year 2)
- Commercial property & corporates (Year 2+)

**Brand personality**
- Precise — not cold
- Confident — not arrogant
- Warm — not casual
- Premium — not exclusive

## 02 · LOGO & MARK

**Logo variants**
- A: Vertical (primary) — VENTRA over coffee, V mark left-aligned
- B: Horizontal (secondary) — use only when vertical does not fit
- C: Reversed — white on black
- D: Mark only — V mark alone, for favicon, social avatar, Xero

**Logo rules**
- Never recolour outside the approved palette
- Never stretch, condense, or distort the wordmark or V mark
- Never use the wordmark without the V mark in primary applications
- Never place on busy photographic backgrounds without a clear field
- Never use any font other than Aptos for the wordmark
- Always maintain clear space of 1× the mark height on all sides

**Mark construction**
- V mark: 32° both sides, butt cap, precise point, stroke = 13% of height
- Wordmark: V mark bottom aligns to top of capitals
- "coffee": Aptos Light 300, 2/3 the size of VENTRA

## 05 · VISUAL LANGUAGE & IMAGERY

**Photography direction**
- Monochrome first — all primary imagery is black and white. Non-negotiable for hero, cover, and proposal use
- Precision framing — tight, considered crops. Machine details, hands with cups, steam, interfaces
- Environment over people — machine, cup, space are the heroes. People as context, not subjects

**Approved image subjects**
- Machine close-ups — Franke, Dr Coffee, Jura machines in clinical detail — always clean, always sharp
- The cup moment — perfect espresso or flat white, placed precisely, steam rising. Never held by a grinning face
- Professional environments — desks, meeting rooms, campus corridors. Clean, ordered, real. B&W treatment always
- Hands at work — operator at a machine, cup collected, phone tapping payment. Action, not personality

**Never use**
- Coffee bags, latte art close-ups, or barista culture imagery
- We are not a café brand

## 07 · TONE OF VOICE

**The four voice principles**

1. **Direct** — Say what we mean in fewer words. No fluff, no preamble, no unnecessary qualifiers. Every word earns its place.
2. **Assured** — Do not convince, do not chase, do not oversell. Confidence is the posture. Competence is the proof.
3. **Human** — Know the client's name, campus, and intake dates. Follow up because we care, not because the CRM said to.
4. **Minimal** — One email when one email is needed. One line when one line is enough. Treat every touchpoint as a privilege.

### Say this / not this

| Say this | Not this |
|---|---|
| We've put together a proposal tailored to your campus size and student numbers. | We're SO excited to share this amazing proposal! |
| Ventra — Proposal for [Institution Name] | Your EXCLUSIVE Coffee Partnership Opportunity Awaits |
| Scheduled maintenance on your Level 3 machine is complete. No action needed. | Hey! Just wanted to reach out and let you know we swung by today! |
| Specialty coffee. Fully managed. One monthly fee. | We're passionate about bringing the coffee shop experience to your workplace! |
| Your agreement renews in 60 days. Are you available Tuesday afternoon? | We absolutely love being your coffee partner! |

## 08 · BRAND INTEGRITY — WHAT NEVER TO DO

### Visual — never
- Use any colour outside the approved palette
- Use fonts other than Aptos and Georgia
- Use saturated colour photography in primary applications
- Use any prior working names (Apex Coffee etc.)
- Reproduce the wordmark without the V mark in primary use
- Place the mark on a busy or textured background

### Communication — never
- Use exclamation points in B2B communications
- Use emoji in client-facing email or proposals
- Use words like: passionate, excited, amazing, innovative
- Send communications without a clear next action
- Refer to ourselves as a "startup" — we are a business
- Make claims we cannot support with data

### Strategy — never
- Position as a café or consumer brand at any point
- Compete on price in the absence of value context
- Over-promise on student FPOS revenue to close a deal
- Use the brand in any consumer-facing context
- Allow roaster partners to co-brand the Ventra identity
- Use Ventra to market to end students — always B2B only
