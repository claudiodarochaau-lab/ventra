The FPOS Revenue Model

The Student Machine Pays for Itself

The student FPOS machine is not a cost item. It is a revenue model. Students tap to pay. Every dollar flows directly to your institution. Ventra takes no cut. At a conservative 15% daily uptake and $3.00 per cup, a campus with 300 enrolled students generates approximately $2,970 per month in student coffee revenue.

Revenue by campus size — illustrative model

Based on 15% daily uptake, $3.00/cup, 22 operating days/month. Conservative assumptions — most campuses exceed 15% within 60 days.

Process

One conversation is enough to get both machines on campus.

01

We assess your campus and recommend the right machine tier

We start with your campus size — staff headcount, enrolled students, and foot traffic profile. From that, we recommend the right machine tier for your staff area and the right placement for your student FPOS machine. No guesswork. No overselling.

02

We install and commission both machines at no upfront cost

Both machines are installed by Ventra — commercial-grade, configured, and ready to use. No setup fees, no equipment purchase, no contractor coordination. We handle the installation. You unlock the door.

03

Your staff and students enjoy great coffee from day one

Your staff area gets a premium bean-to-cup machine with specialty beans. Your students tap to pay at a contactless terminal in the common room. Revenue from every student cup flows directly to your institution.

04

We handle everything. You receive one monthly invoice.

Maintenance, consumables, auto-replenishment, and service response are all on us. 24–48 hour response guarantee. You don't file a request, manage a supplier, or think about coffee. One invoice. That is the arrangement.
