How It Works

Simple from day one.

Five steps. One partner. Nothing to manage.

Choose

Pick your setup.

Tell us your location, daily volume, and space. We'll recommend the right machine for your throughput — no upselling, no over-speccing.

Install

We handle installation.

Ventra installs, configures, and commissions the machine. You sign off when it's ready. That's the last time you'll need to think about it.

Replenish

Beans arrive before you run out.

Curated specialty roasts are auto-replenished to your schedule. Restaurant-quality coffee, every morning — with no ordering, no tracking, no running out.

Invoice

One invoice. Fixed monthly.

A single line item covers your machine, your beans, and your maintenance. No surprise charges. No variable costs unless you order above your monthly allowance.

Revenue OPTIONAL

A cashless payment terminal turns every cup into a transaction. Revenue flows directly to you — Ventra takes no cut. At scale, this offsets your full monthly cost.
