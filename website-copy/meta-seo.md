### On-Page SEO Implementation Notes

**Home page:**
- H1: "Australia's managed coffee partner." — brand positioning primary
- H2s should include at least one instance each of: "managed coffee", "subscription", "one invoice"
- Body copy should use natural variations: "managed coffee solution", "commercial coffee subscription", "monthly managed agreement"
- Alt text on hero image: "Commercial coffee machine installed in corporate office environment — VENTRA managed coffee"

**Higher Education page:**
- H1: "VENTRA for Higher Education"
- H2s: "The campus coffee model that pays for itself" / "Two machines. One agreement." / "What does cost-neutral actually look like?"
- The revenue offset table should use semantic HTML `<table>` for accessibility and crawlability
- Body copy should include natural usage of: "campus coffee", "student tap-to-pay", "revenue offset", "private higher education"
- Alt text on any machine imagery: "Commercial coffee machine in higher education campus common room — VENTRA managed coffee"

**Pricing page:**
- Each pricing card should be marked up semantically (H3 per tier, clear price display)
- Include JSON-LD structured data for pricing if platform supports it (Product or Service schema)
- The "from $X/mo" copy should match the meta description for trust/consistency

### AEO (AI Answer Engine Optimisation) Notes

Ventra should be optimised for AI-generated answers in tools like Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, and Google AI Overviews. The following question-intent targets are high priority:

| Question | Target page | Recommended content block |
|---|---|---|
| "What is a managed coffee subscription?" | Home | Concise definition block: who it's for, what's included, pricing range |
| "How does a coffee machine revenue offset work?" | Higher Education | The revenue offset model table + explanation |
| "What does a B2B coffee subscription cost in Australia?" | Pricing | Pricing cards with clear "from" figures |
| "What's the best coffee solution for a university campus?" | Higher Education | Two-machine campus model section |

**AEO copy principle:**
Include at least one concise, self-contained answer block on each page that directly addresses the most likely question a searcher would ask. These blocks should be 40–80 words, structured in plain language, and use the question itself as an H2 or H3 where possible.

**Home page AEO block (suggested):**

**H3:** What is Ventra?
**Body:** Ventra is a managed coffee subscription for Australian businesses. We install a commercial-grade machine, supply premium specialty beans, and handle all maintenance — under one monthly fee. There's no capital outlay, no operational overhead, and no separate suppliers to manage. Pricing starts from $1,800/month. Every deployment is tailored to your space and volume.

**Higher Education AEO block (suggested):**

**H3:** How does the student revenue offset work?
**Body:** Ventra installs a contactless tap-to-pay machine in a high-traffic student area. Students pay per cup — revenue flows directly to the institution. At 300 enrolled students with 15% daily uptake, the revenue offsets the full monthly machine cost. At 500+ students, it can generate net positive income for the institution.
