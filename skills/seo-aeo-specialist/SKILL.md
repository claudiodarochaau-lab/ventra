## AEO Principle: Writing for Machines That Summarise

AI answer engines don't just index pages — they extract the most citable, authoritative snippet from a page and present it as an answer. This means:

- **Lead with the answer** — don't bury the key claim in paragraph 4
- **Use clear definitional structures** — "X is Y" / "The three steps to Z are..."
- **Use structured headers** that match likely query phrasing
- **Cite specifics** — dates, statistics, named entities, regulatory references (e.g. National Code, CRICOS) signal authority
- **Schema everything** — FAQ schema on every FAQ block, Article schema on every blog post, Organization schema on every page
- **Build entity coherence** — Ventra should appear consistently across its own site, LinkedIn, Google Business Profile, and any media mentions so AI systems can confidently identify and cite it
